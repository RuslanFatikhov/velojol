About Velojol

Velojol is the result of a passion for bicycles and a desire to make Almaty's urban life more sustainable and green. Our project started as an idea: to provide residents and guests of the city with up-to-date and detailed information about bicycle routes in Almaty. Over time, it grew into a community of bicycle enthusiasts whose goal is to make the city bicycle friendly

Our mission

We aim to create an integrated map of bicycle lanes in Almaty that will be accessible to everyone. Our mission is to make cycling convenient, safe and fun. We want to inspire people to live an active lifestyle by promoting cycling culture and reducing environmental impact

Overall assessment of the city

Bicycle lanes in Almaty represent a diverse infrastructure that extends throughout the city. They include separated and sidewalk routes along former streetcar tracks and parks. The extensive network of bicycle lanes is marked with brightly colored markings and signs, ensuring safety for cyclists.

The bicycle lane surfaces are mostly of good quality, but there are some sections with unstable or worn pavement, which can be inconvenient for cyclists. Some sections also have lighting problems, making them less safe at night.

Bicycle lanes in Almaty extend from main streets to outlying areas, providing convenience and accessibility for many of the city's residents. They intersect with other bicycle routes and parallel streets, providing cyclists with a variety of routes to choose from.

Despite some shortcomings, bicycle lanes in Almaty play an important role in the development of cycling culture in the city. With a constant focus on improving pavement quality and safety, they continue to serve as a convenient means of transportation for many residents, promoting an active and healthy lifestyle.
